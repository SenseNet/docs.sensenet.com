## SetInitialPreviewProperties
- Method: **POST**.


### Request example:

```
POST /odata.svc/Root/...('targetContent')/SetInitialPreviewProperties
```
The `targetContent` can be PreviewImage
### Parameters:
There are no parameters.

### Requirements:
- **AllowedRoles**: Everyone
